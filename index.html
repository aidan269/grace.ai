How it works

One URL. Everything else is Grace.

01 Research
Reads and thinks
Fetches the article, gathers community intelligence from X and infosec forums, and scores the threat on novelty, named actors, and impact.
02 Generate
Writes the plugin
Produces a complete cantinasec plugin with detection steps, IOCs, risk classification, and remediation. All sections. No blanks.
03 Ship
Asks, then pushes
Creates the repo, commits everything, and waits for your go-ahead before pushing to GitHub. You stay in the loop.
